This Leave Policy outlines the leave entitlements, types of leave, eligibility, and procedures for employees using TEL (Track Employee Location), developed by Crrescita Innovators Private Limited. It applies to all employees and should be read along with your employment contract and company handbook.

Purpose

This policy aims to ensure fair, consistent, and transparent handling of leave requests while supporting work-life balance and business continuity. Leave must be applied and approved through the designated process (including via TEL where applicable) before being availed.

Types of Leave

Employees may be eligible for the following types of leave:

  • Earned Leave / Privilege Leave (EL/PL): As per company policy and applicable law; typically accrued based on tenure and usage.
  • Sick Leave (SL): For medical reasons; may require supporting documents as per company rules.
  • Casual Leave (CL): Short-duration leave for personal reasons, subject to company limits and approval.
  • Maternity / Paternity Leave: As per statutory provisions and company policy.
  • Bereavement Leave: For family bereavement, as defined by the company.
  • Leave Without Pay (LWP): When no other leave is available or applicable; subject to approval.
Eligibility and Accrual

Leave eligibility and accrual are governed by your employment terms, tenure, and applicable labour laws. Specific numbers of days for each leave type will be as per your offer letter, company policy, or HR communication. Unused leave may be carried forward or encashed only as per company policy and local regulations.

Applying for Leave

Employees must apply for leave in advance through the official channel (e.g., TEL app, HR portal, or email as specified by the company). Applications should include leave type, dates, and reason where required. For unplanned leave (e.g., sick leave), inform your manager and HR at the earliest and submit documents as per policy.

Approval and Records

Leave becomes valid only after approval by the designated authority. Approved leave will be recorded in company systems (including TEL where used for attendance and location tracking). Unapproved leave may be treated as absent and could attract disciplinary action as per company policy.

Cancellation and Modification

Requests to cancel or modify approved leave must be communicated to your manager and HR as per company process. Approval of such requests is at the discretion of the company based on operational requirements.

Leave and Attendance in TEL

When you are on approved leave, your attendance and location tracking in TEL will be aligned with leave records. You are not required to mark attendance or share location for days that are officially recorded as leave, unless otherwise specified by your employer.

Encashment and Carry-Forward

Rules for encashment of unused leave and carry-forward to the next year are defined by your company and applicable law. Please refer to your HR policy or handbook for exact entitlements and cut-off dates.

Misuse of Leave

Misrepresentation of leave type, dates, or reason, or availing leave without approval, may be treated as misconduct and could result in disciplinary action as per company policy.

Changes to This Policy

Crrescita Innovators Private Limited (or your employer, as the case may be) may update this leave policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through appropriate channels. Continued employment implies acceptance of the policy as updated.
