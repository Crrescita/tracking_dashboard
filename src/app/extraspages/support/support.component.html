What is TEL?

TEL is a comprehensive solution for managing employee attendance and location tracking, designed specifically for organizations with field teams.

Why does TEL need access to my location?

TEL requires location access to accurately track attendance and work hours, ensuring operational efficiency and accountability.

How is my location data used?

Your location data is used solely for tracking attendance and work hours, even when the app is closed or not in use.

Do I have to provide location access?

Yes, providing location access is mandatory to ensure a smooth check-in and check-out process. Location sharing is required to verify your presence at the designated site or area, ensuring compliance with company policies or operational requirements. Granting location access helps to accurately record attendance and streamline the check-in process.

What happens if I deny location access?

If you deny location access, the app will not be able to track your attendance accurately, which may result in missing work hour records.

Can I revoke location access after giving consent?

Yes, you can revoke location access at any time through your device settings. However, this may impact the app’s ability to track attendance.

Is my location data shared with third parties?

No, your location data is not shared with any third parties. It is used solely for internal attendance management purposes.

How can I view my attendance history?

You can view your attendance history easily through the Attendance Report feature in your profile

What if I encounter issues while using the app?

You can submit support requests directly through the app for quick assistance.
