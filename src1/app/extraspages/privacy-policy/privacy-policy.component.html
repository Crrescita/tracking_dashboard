Data Categories

Clients' Contact Information

We collect information such as the name, phone number, and email of field employees' clients for app functionality, only after obtaining the necessary permissions from our customers.

Clients' GPS Location

We collect location-based data (GPS coordinates) through our geolocation feature to record employee visits with their GPS location.

Clients' Images

We collect company images if a client has signed up for this feature and agreed to its use. This feature is optional and only available to those who opt in. We do not share collected images with third parties, nor use them for purposes other than those disclosed. Images are stored for the duration of the client's subscription and are automatically deleted 365 days after the service ends.

We may process data for business management, reporting, and analysis to provide business services, analytics, and business intelligence.
